About Me

Hi! My name is Rishibh Prakash and I am a Master's student at the University of Toronto studying mathematics. I also did my undergraduate at the University of Toronto in mathematics (specialist) and computer science (major). I find the vast majority of things interesting and am always excited to learn more.
